   As the weather cools it&#8217;s easy to get into the habit of eating lots of rather stodgy (albeit tasty) food. This is a great side salad which works to rectify the stodge and and add great flavour and texture to a meal.



  



  <i>Ingredients:</i>


<li class="p2">
  1/2 red onion, finely diced
</li>
<li class="p2">
  1 capsicum, cored and diced
</li>
<li class="p2">
  1 bunch radishes, finely sliced
</li>
<li class="p2">
  1/2 cup basil leaves, washed and roughly chopped
</li>
<li class="p2">
  1 cup baby tomatoes, halved
</li>
<li class="p2">
  1 cucumber, diced
</li>
<li class="p2">
  1 carrot, diced
</li>
<li class="p2">
  1 cup walnuts, roughly chopped
</li>

Salad dressing:

<li class="p2">
  1 garlic clove, finely diced
</li>
<li class="p2">
  1/4 cup white wine vinegar
</li>
<li class="p2">
  1 diced spring onion
</li>
<li class="p2">
  2 tablespoons walnut oil
</li>
<li class="p2">
  1 tablespoons agave syrup
</li>
<li class="p2">
  1 tablespoon lemon juice
</li>
<li class="p2">
  <span class="s1">½ teaspoon salt
</li>
<li class="p2">
  <span class="s1">½ teaspoon black pepper
</li>


  <i>Instructions:</i>


<ol class="ol1">
  <li class="li2">
    Combine salad ingredients in a bowl.
  </li>
  <li class="li2">
    Combine salad dressing ingredients in a <a href="atc-migrate/2015/11/IMG_20150720_101749.jpg"><br /> </a>jar and shake well. Pour onto salad 15 minutes before serving.
  </li>
</ol>
